About Us

WELCOME to Waffle House! We’re all about serving tasty homemade waffles made from local, fresh ingredients and innovative toppings that bring smiles and warmth in every serving.

From classics to brand-new flavor profiles, our menu is designed with all cravings in mind — whether it's for a hearty breakfast, casual brunch, or indulgent afternoon treat.

Waffle House started in 1995 as a small family bakery. The idea was to create the perfect waffle experience using locally sourced ingredients. Over the years, we’ve grown to become a cozy café beloved by our community.
